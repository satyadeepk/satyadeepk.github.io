Notes on Compiler:

"The author is Hindupur Avinash, a final year B.Tech student from Department of Biotechnology who is an enthusiastic bird watcher and has been trying to make an exhaustive list of birds in campus in the last four years. He has been documenting his sightings in his website sillyreflections.com. He has also created a checklist of all the 125 species of birds recorded within the campus with classification according to their families"
