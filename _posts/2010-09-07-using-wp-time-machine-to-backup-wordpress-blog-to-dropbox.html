Having personal backups of your blog is a necessity even if your hosting provider is assuring automatic backups. Backup requires downloading both the wordpress files and the mysql database dump.

Wouldn't it be nice to automatically get the backup of your whole blog  (database + files)  into your Dropbox. Wp Time Machine wordpress plugin does just that. The Wp Time Machine plugin also gives the instructions for easy restoration of the blog.

Install the plugin. Give your Dropbox login details in the settings page and the backup is sent to your Dropbox folder. You can also schedule the backups on a weekly or a daily basis by running the plugin using cron jobs.

If you don't know what Dropbox is, it is a file synchronization and backup tool which automatically syncs all your computers which are linked to your Dropbox to the cloud. It also gives a 2 GB free online storage space.
